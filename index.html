Welcome to INSILICO INSIGHTS

Cutting-edge cheminformatics and AI-driven solutions for drug discovery.

INSILICO INSIGHTS is a consulting firm specializing in computational chemistry, cheminformatics, and AI-based approaches for modern drug discovery and material design. We deliver innovative solutions through predictive modeling, molecular docking, and data analytics.

Founded by Dr. Yassir Boulaamane, our mission is to empower research teams and industries with cutting-edge computational tools, enabling faster and more efficient scientific breakthroughs.

OUR SERVICES

We specialize in these areas:

QSAR Modeling: Predictive analysis for bioactivity and ADMET properties using machine learning techniques.

Molecular Docking: Virtual screening and binding affinity predictions for lead compounds.

Molecular Dynamics Simulations: Structural stability and interaction studies of biomolecules.

AI-Driven Drug Discovery: Machine learning models for hit-to-lead optimization and data integration.
